Our philosophy

"Consistency doesn't mean
showing up at your best.
It means showing up."

Most fitness apps are built around pushing you harder. Pacein is different. It asks how you feel first — then builds your day around that answer. No guilt. No streaks to protect. Just movement that makes sense for today.

How it works

Five minutes of honesty.
A day of movement.

No planning required. Just check in and Pacein handles the rest.

01
Morning check-in

A gentle notification asks how you're feeling. No right answer — just honest ones. Low, meh, good, or energised.

02
Adaptive session

Pacein generates a personalised 5–30 minute movement session that matches your energy and time today.

03
Calm guidance

Move through your session with clear, unhurried guidance. No countdown pressure. No performance metrics.

04
It learns & adapts

After each session, Pacein quietly learns your patterns and makes tomorrow's plan a little more you.

Features

Everything it needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Mood-based sessions

Each day's movement is generated around how you actually feel — not an arbitrary schedule someone else made.

AI that listens

The more you use Pacein, the better it understands your patterns. Your energy cycles, your busy weeks, your best hours.

Insight dashboard

See your movement patterns over time. Not to judge — but to understand yourself better and notice what actually helps.

Smart notifications

Reminders that respect your energy. Pacein learns the best time to reach you — and backs off when you need space.

5–30 min sessions

Short enough to always be possible. Long enough to always be meaningful. The session fits your day, not the other way around.

Calm voice & tone

No "crush it" energy here. Pacein speaks like a thoughtful friend — observant, gentle, and genuinely on your side.
